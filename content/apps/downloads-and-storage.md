Playing a book is as simple as tapping **Play**. Behind the scenes, ListenUp downloads and saves the file to your device, so the next time you open that book it starts instantly, and plays even with no connection.

## Downloading for offline

Next to each book's Play button is a **download** control that shows the book's download status. Once the book is fully downloaded, it turns into a **delete** button.

If you delete a download and then play the book again, ListenUp takes the hint: it assumes you'd rather not keep that one on your device, so it streams it instead of silently re-downloading. Want it offline again? Just tap the **download** icon.

> [!TIP]
> Heading somewhere without a connection? Download what you want to hear first. Anything already on your device plays fully offline.

## Managing storage

The **Storage** page shows how much space ListenUp is using and lists every book currently downloaded to your device, and you can delete downloads from there too.

- **Android**: open it from the **profile dropdown**.
- **iOS**: open it from the link on your **profile page**.

## When you finish a book

Finish a book and ListenUp removes its download automatically, freeing up space for your next listen.
