A **shelf** is a list of books you put together yourself: "Up Next," "Favorites," a book-club reading list, whatever's useful. Shelves are about organization, not access: adding a book to a shelf never changes who can see it. (That's what [Collections](/admin/collections/) are for.)

## Creating a shelf

You can make a shelf two ways:

- From your **profile** page.
- From any book, tap **Add to Shelf**.

## Public or private

Each shelf can be **public** or **private**. Keep one to yourself, or make it public to share your list with other members of your server. Handy for recommendations or a book club.

> [!NOTE]
> Shelves and [collections](/admin/collections/) look similar but do different jobs: a **shelf** is a personal list any member can make, while a **collection** is an admin tool for controlling who can access which books.
